Hi! I am currently a Ph.D. student at Yonsei University, advised by Prof. Hyung-Chan An. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Yonsei University.

My major research interests reside in theoretical computer science. I am particularly interested in (but not limited to) online algorithms, approximation algorithms, and learning-augmented algorithms.

I am also eager to convey theoretical aspects of computer science to others, specifically in Korean. To this end, I am running a blog where I post several related topics including basics to recent work. Visit my blog 「Gazelle and Computer Science」 (in Korean) if interested.
